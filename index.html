BRIGHT CS

This project was started by Kimi and Amina in an effort to equalize the school system

Many students are unaware of the inequality present in the APS community due to its presence in everyday life

We designed this project to make these inequalities public and available for everyone to witness and possibly make better

Here we will make known the opportunities that APS has been keeping away from families of APS that are economically-disadvantaged or families where their first language is not English
